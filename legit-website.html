Minecraft - Reincarnated

Minecraft is back as one of the most popular games ever after the collapse of fortnite, which was fated to die anyway. YouTuber Felix Kjellberg, better known as PewDiePie, was able to revive not only his channel but an entire video game by uploading a simple Let’s Play: “Minecraft Part 1.” This also revived Minecraft, which had stayed ignored for a few years. Now minecraft is back, we are able to look back at the history and development of minecraft
